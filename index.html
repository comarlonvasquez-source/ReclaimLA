FAQ

Common
questions.

How does pricing work?

+
We give you a full upfront quote before any work begins. Pricing is based on load size and item weight. Heavy items like appliances and concrete may carry an additional charge, which we'll always communicate first. No surprises, ever.

Do you really buy items?

+
Yes. We buy unwanted furniture, appliances, and valuables directly from you. Instead of just paying to have things hauled away, you may walk away with cash. We assess items on arrival based on condition and resale value.

How quickly can you come out?

+
We offer same-week availability across Santa Monica and West LA. Book online or text us at (310) 957-1362 and we'll get you scheduled fast.

Do you help with organization too?

+
Yes — beyond cleanouts we offer full home organization, storage setup, shelving installation, and deep cleaning. Once the space is clear we help you make it truly functional and organized.

What areas do you serve?

+
We serve Santa Monica and the greater West LA area. Not sure if we cover your neighborhood? Text us and we'll let you know right away.

What happens to my stuff after?

+
Items we purchase are individually resold. Everything else is hauled to a licensed disposal facility. Electronics and paint are handled separately per California regulations.
